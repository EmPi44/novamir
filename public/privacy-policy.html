Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Data, Respecting Your Trust

We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our platforms.

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Who We Are

Novamir ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for your personal information. We're based at:

Meydan Road, Nad Al Sheba, Dubai, U.A.E.
Contact us at hello@novamir.ai.

Why We Use Your Data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Lead Qualification – to assess interest and respond to inquiries.
  • Marketing Communications – to keep you informed about our offerings.
  • Website Analytics – to analyze usage trends, improve performance, and detect abuse.

What Data We Collect

Automatically collected data ("Device Information"):

  • Includes browser type, IP address, device identifiers, time zone, and usage metrics like pages visited or referral source.

Voluntarily submitted data:

  • Includes your name, email, phone number, or any other data you provide via contact or lead forms.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your Consent – when you provide data voluntarily for specific purposes.
  • Our Legitimate Interests – such as improving our service, analyzing usage patterns, or protecting our systems, so long as those interests don't override your rights.

Your Rights

Under both UAE PDPL and EU GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request deletion ("right to erasure")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@novamir.ai. We will respond promptly.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes or comply with legal obligations. You may also request deletion at any time; we will fulfill deletion requests without undue delay, unless required to retain data by law.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the UAE or EU (e.g., to service providers like CRM platforms or analytics tools). We use appropriate safeguards—such as contractual agreements—to ensure continued protection aligned with UAE PDPL and EU GDPR standards.

Security & Breach Notification

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights or freedom, we will notify you and the relevant UAE authority (Data Office) promptly, as required by law.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external sites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies independently.

Use of WhatsApp Business Platform & Meta Products

As part of our services, we use the WhatsApp Business Platform (powered by Meta) to support automated and human-assisted lead qualification, communication, and customer engagement on behalf of our clients.

When you contact us or our clients through WhatsApp, we may process the following types of personal data:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number, name, profile picture, and language setting
  • Your messages, including text, media (images, audio, video), documents, and attachments
  • Message metadata, including timestamps, message status (sent, delivered, read), and conversation IDs
  • Interaction logs used for performance monitoring, follow-ups, analytics, or service improvement

This data is processed to enable two-way messaging, automate common inquiries, assess lead quality, and pass qualified leads to our clients' teams.

We may also interact with other Meta developer tools or APIs (e.g., for templates, analytics, account management) as part of delivering the service.

Data Sharing and Responsibility

Your messages and data may be shared with:

  • Meta Platforms Inc., which provides the WhatsApp Business Platform. Meta processes this data under its own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • Our Clients, when we act as a processor or integration provider on their behalf (e.g., real estate agencies, brokers, or sales teams you contact via WhatsApp).
  • Our Service Providers, such as CRM, cloud hosting, or analytics tools, solely for the purpose of delivering the service.

We do not sell WhatsApp conversation data or use it for profiling unrelated to the original purpose.

Your Control & Rights (WhatsApp)

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your WhatsApp conversation data with us or our clients at any time by emailing hello@novamir.ai. If you no longer wish to be contacted via WhatsApp, reply with "STOP", and we will immediately cease communication.

We will only retain WhatsApp data as long as needed to fulfill service obligations or client instructions, unless longer retention is legally required.

Compliance with WhatsApp Business Solution Terms

No Use for AI Model Training

We confirm that we do not use WhatsApp Business Solution Data (including messages, metadata, or derived data) to create, develop, train, or improve any large-language models or general-purpose AI models. In the event that we use AI/ML for internal automation, such models are strictly for our exclusive use and do not incorporate Business Solution Data beyond the specific conversation context.

Third-Party Service Provider Safeguards

When we engage service providers (e.g., CRM, hosting, analytics, or AI/ML vendors) that process your WhatsApp messages or conversation data on our behalf, we ensure via contract that they:

  • Process that data only on our instructions and for our clients' defined purposes,
  • Comply with applicable data protection terms including WhatsApp Business Solution Terms, and
  • Maintain safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

No Profiling or Commercial Exploitation

We do not track, build, or augment user profiles using WhatsApp data beyond what is needed to serve and respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, license, or otherwise distribute WhatsApp data for unrelated purposes.

Controller vs Processor Role

Where we act on behalf of a client (e.g., a real estate agency), that client remains the data controller and we act as their processor. We process the data only on their documented instructions, and the client is responsible for ensuring they have a valid legal basis to process personal data.

This policy reflects our alignment with the WhatsApp Business Solution Terms.
