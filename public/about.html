About Novamir

AI Systems That Reclaim Your Time

Who We Are

Novamir is a team of AI engineers and product strategists based in Dubai, U.A.E. We partner with forward-thinking businesses to design, build, and deploy AI-powered automation that drives measurable results.

We've eliminated over 10,000 hours of manual work across our client base with scalable AI systems — hours reclaimed for revenue, customers, and core decisions.

What We Do

We work alongside organizations to identify high-impact opportunities for automation. Whether it's sales outreach, customer support, content generation, or operational workflows, we build systems that scale with the business and adapt to changing needs.

  • Gini — An autonomous AI sales agent for WhatsApp that handles outreach, follow-ups, and lead qualification around the clock.
  • Henry — An intelligent customer service voice agent that understands context, resolves inquiries instantly, and escalates only when it matters.
  • Custom Solutions — Purpose-built AI platforms tailored to specific business challenges.

Our Approach

Every engagement starts with a deep-dive discovery session to understand unique challenges and goals. We design a custom AI roadmap, build and test the solution, and provide ongoing support to ensure long-term success. Our process is collaborative, transparent, and focused on delivering ROI from day one.

We follow a four-phase methodology: Discovery, where we map your workflows and identify bottlenecks; Design, where we architect the right AI solution; Build, where we develop and rigorously test every component; and Optimize, where we monitor performance and continuously improve results based on real-world data.

Trusted By

Innovative companies including Resapass, Dronicle, Umami Comms, Pods and Spices, and Missions of Honor trust Novamir to transform their operations with AI. In collaboration with Genie AI Solutions, we bring together the best minds in artificial intelligence to solve real business problems.
